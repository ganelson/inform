  Experimentation Chamber
  Robo, your prototype tin companion, stands awkwardly beside you. In the middle of its chest is a red enamel button, currently un-depressed.
  
  You can also see a red block and a blue cylinder here.
  
  Robo watches you looking. In its metal head, gears whirr.

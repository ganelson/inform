^^{Status line with centered text, the easy way}

If we want to lay out the status line in some other way than with left-hand and right-hand entries, it is possible to do this as well. Later we will learn about the `rule for constructing the status line`, but here is a basic effect using this rule and the `draw the status window with (table)` phrase.

	{*}"Centered"
	
	When play begins:
		say "After months of boring through the Earth's crust in this metal-jawed vehicle, you break through..."
	
	The Hollow Core is a room. "Truly a magnificent sight: the land curves up away from you in every direction, covered with the cities and fields of the Core People. Molten rock runs in the canals, bringing heat and light to every home.
	
	At the center of the Earth hangs a dense black sun."
	
	Table of Centred Status
	middle
	"[location]"
	
	Rule for constructing the status line:
		draw the status window with the Table of Centred Status;
	
	Test me with "look".
